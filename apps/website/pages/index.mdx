**Zh** is a plugins collection designed to optimize Chinese text formatting.

## Features

- **spaceAroundAlphabet**: add spaces around Chinese and English characters.
- **spaceAroundNumber**: add spaces around Chinese and Number characters.
- **noSpaceBetweenNumberUnit**: clear spaces between numbers and specified unit symbols.
- In the future, more formatting options for Chinese text will be added.

## Contributing
